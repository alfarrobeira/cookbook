Borsch

Everyone knows what it is and many people around the world have fallen in love with this iconic beet soup.
Ukrainian kitchen

For 10 servings

Time you need 1,5h

Ingredients

For Borscht: 3 medium beets, peeled and grated 4 Tbsp olive oil, divided 8 cups chicken broth , + 2 cups water 3 medium yukon potatoes, peeled and sliced into bite-sized pieces 2 carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
For Zazharka (Mirepoix): 2 celery ribs, trimmed and finely chopped 1 small red bell pepper, finely chopped, optional 1 medium onion, finely chopped 4 Tbsp ketchup or 3 Tbsp tomato sauce
Additional Flavorings: 1 can white cannelini beans with their juice 2 bay leaves 2-3 Tbsp white vinegar, or to taste 1 tsp sea salt, or to taste 1/4 tsp black pepper, freshly ground 1 large garlic clove, pressed 3 Tbsp chopped dill
